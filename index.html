About the Shelter

"From Strays to Stays"

“From Strays to Stays” is a non-profit organization that rescues homeless and abandoned animals and provides them with shelter, food, medical care, and adoption services.

Their mission is to give every animal a chance to find a loving and permanent home, and to reduce the number of unwanted pets in the community. They operate in North York, Ontario, Canada, and rely on donations and volunteers to support their work. They also offer educational programs and spay/neuter services to promote responsible pet ownership and animal welfare.
